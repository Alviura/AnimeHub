Anime

Television genre:


Anime is a hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan. Outside Japan and in English, anime refers specifically to animation produced in Japan. However, in Japan and in Japanese, anime describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin.

~Wikipedia


Here are some of my Favourites

Naruto: The tale of a spirited young ninja with a sealed power, navigates friendship and challanges on his quest to become the strongest and earn respect as Hokage.

Demon Slayer: Tanjiro Kamado battles demons, seeking a cure for his sister. Stunning animation, intense fights, and a quest for humanity's survival.

Vinland Saga: Epic tale of a Viking warrior Thorfinn's quest for vengeance and self- discovery during the tumultuous Viking Age.

Seven Deadly Sins: Meliodas leads a group of powerful knights on a quest to save Britannia from evil. Adventure, magic and epic battles unfold in this captivating anime.
